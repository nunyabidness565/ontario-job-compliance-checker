Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Summary

We do not collect, store, or transmit your job posting content or personal data. By default this site uses no analytics, no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no third-party tracking cookies.

Local browser storage

To make the tool more convenient, your job posting text and salary inputs are saved in your browser's localStorage on your own device. This data never leaves your browser. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear" button or by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-party assets

This site loads Tailwind CSS from a public content delivery network (CDN) so that styles render correctly. Your browser will make a request to that CDN, which may receive standard request information (such as your IP address) as part of normal web operation. We do not control or receive that data.

Cookies

We do not set cookies for tracking, profiling, or advertising.

Children

This tool is intended for adult professional use and is not directed at children.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
