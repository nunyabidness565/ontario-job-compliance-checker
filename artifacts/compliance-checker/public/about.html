About

The Ontario Job Posting Compliance Checker is a free, browser-based tool that helps Ontario recruiters quickly review job postings against the publicly advertised job posting rules introduced by Bill 149 (Working for Workers Four Act).

Purpose

Ontario has tightened transparency rules for job postings — including expected compensation ranges, disclosure of artificial intelligence used in hiring, and restrictions on requiring "Canadian experience". Many recruiters need a fast sanity check before publishing. This tool gives you a Red / Orange / Green scorecard in seconds, with plain-English guidance.

Who it's for

  • In-house Ontario recruiters and talent teams
  • Agency recruiters posting roles on behalf of Ontario employers
  • Hiring managers writing their own job ads
  • HR and compliance leads reviewing posting templates

How it works

  1. Paste your job posting text into the editor.
  2. Enter the expected annual minimum and maximum salary in CAD.
  3. The tool checks for: (a) salary range spread, (b) the prohibited phrase "Canadian experience", and (c) AI / automated screening disclosure.
  4. You get a Red / Orange / Green status for each item, plus an overall score.
  5. Use the "Insert AI Disclosure Template" button to add a starter disclosure if needed.

All checks run entirely in your browser. Your text and salary inputs are saved only in your browser's localStorage on your own device. No content is sent to a server.
