Frequently asked questions

2026 Ontario rules, in plain English.

Does the $50,000 salary spread rule apply to all jobs?

No. If the salary is over $200,000, the spread limit does not apply.

Is AI disclosure required on internal postings?

Bill 149 specifically targets "publicly advertised" job postings.

Can I still ask for "local experience"?

"Canadian experience" is strictly prohibited, but specific regulatory or local knowledge may still be permitted.
